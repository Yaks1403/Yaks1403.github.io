Threats to the Enviroment

The two threats we will be discussing on this website are Overpopulation and Deforestation.

Human Overpopulation has a huge effect on the envrioment, from having to cut down rainforests to dumping plastics and chemicals in the ocean.
Deforestation and Overpopulation are linked and are often the cause of each other. This is because when we cut down large parts of a rainforest or forest we are usally doing in for land to build housing, agricutural land or for other things such as palm oil and vegatable oil.
